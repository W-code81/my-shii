MADE IN ABYSS




Overview

MIA is a game created by the Dami Brothers .It captures the life of 2 children exploring a
mysterious gigantic hole called the abyss and they try to uncover its mysteries while trying
to survive the monsters in the deep. It's inspired by the video game The Last Of Us and the
anime Made In Abyss, it's an open-world game with a large map and it falls under sci-fi,
adventure, mystery and action. I know it's gonna be a great game when it's out for download.


Game Specs

It is important to know the specification demanded from your system in order to get the best
result in speed,graphic quaity and overall game play. The specs include the following;

RAM: 8.00 GB recommended, 4 GB minimum
DX: Microsoft DirectX 9.0c
OS: Microsoft Windows XP, Windows 8-11
SOUND: 100% DirectX 9.0c compatible sound card
SYSTEM: 64-bit operating system, x64-based processor
GAME SIZE: 20 GB
PROCESSOR: 2.60 GHz or higher
